1. BAND UP

Band Up is my senior projects that i have done with my colleagues were we developed a website using Next.js, Supabase, and Prisma to connect musicians together and help facilitate collaboration between each other and offering them a safe and encouraging place where they can share their talents and get seeked out by scouts.

2. STRANDED

I developed a science fiction game utilizing Unreal Engine 4. In this game, players embark on a captivating journey through immersive levels, assuming the role of a lost protagonist and facing the challenge of survival.

3. DELIRIUM

Delirium is a thrilling horror game that was done using Unreal Engine 5, challenging players to survive, escape, and strategically complete objectives while evading a roaming entity. that is slowly creeping closer waiting for the perfect chance to strike.
